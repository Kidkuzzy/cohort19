As you can see, a proposal generally consists of: Introduction: A brief overview of the problem, solution, costs, and benefits. Issue: The main definition of the issue, including subject, purpose, main argument, background information and importance. Solution: The main definition of the solution, including your step-by-step plan, the benefits, and how potential obstacles will be overcame. Qualifications: Overview of the personnel required, experience. Conclusion of the costs and benefits, and wrap-up: Balance the cost against the benefit, reinforce your point one last time.
